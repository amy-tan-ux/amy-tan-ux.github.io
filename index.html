Codes and Demos
Random Music Generator
This Random Music Generator utilizes a database of Music Theory to generate the next note. A bounded knapsack algorithm was used to generate rhythms for each bar.
Math Arithemetic Game
This basic Math Arithemetic Game generates math expressions according to the level of the player. Every right answer adds to the total score of the player, advancing the player's level each time. Wrong answers loses scores, increases math problems per level and/or demotes the player to a lower level.
Bike Rider Analysis
Bike Rider Data is analyzed to determine the trend of bike users over time. Using clusters the bike user demographics were identified.
Billboard Top Songs Database
In this project I designed a relational database using mySQLite that stores information of Music that charted on the billboard top 10, the artists and other relevant information about the songs.
